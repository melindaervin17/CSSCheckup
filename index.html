Panda Facts

Here are some fun facts about pandas!

  • Diet mainly consists of bamboo, but will occasionally eat other vegetation, fish and small animals
  • Excellent tree-climbers and swimmers
  • Have enlarged wrist bones that act like thumbs for gripping
  • Average lifespan is 20 years in the wild
  • Not-so-fun fact: Females that give birth to twins will often abandon the weaker of the two to take care of the stronger one
